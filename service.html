What is Bling On

The action we aim to offer to address the aforementioned problem is by introducing our project called “Bling On” a social entrepreneurship that we introduce to the institution, by doing this we can make this proposed project more sustainable and even though we are not assigned on this institution anymore the Padre Pio can still use it to solve the lack of donations, behavioral issues and academic issues of the children and to also spread awareness. Starting off, We intend to teach and create fun and educational activities for each children to learn and by making recyclable bracelets, pot and christmas parol, and others that the children can use to sell and profit from. not only can this help with the financial issues Padre Pio is currenlty dealing but it can also show their creativeness

Outcomes

1. The primary result is that the Padre Pio Intuition will have enough funds to meet the needs and desires of the children; additionally, the children of Padre Pio will be able to strengthen their academic and behavioral issues by the end of the proposal.

2. By raising awareness, We will double the number of donors. Increase the level of academic achievement of Padre Pio children by 30

Objectives

The main goal of the project is to sell and fund enough money for the intuition and fix the behavioral issues of the children, by doing this we can help them solve their worries and give them a clear future with no worries. The specific objectives include:

To have enough funding of to provide the intuition.
To be able to promote our project proposal all over the Ateneo Campus
To gather the resources or materials needed for the proposal.
